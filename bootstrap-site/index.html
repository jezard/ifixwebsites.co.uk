Priorotise website maintenance? Why?

There are many reasons why you should update your website, here are just three:

  • A broken website does not give a good impression of your business
  • A un-updated website is be more likely to be successfully attacked
  • Technology changes rapidly over time, don't get left behind - give your visitors the best user experience
